Tacos

Ingredients

The following Recipe is for 4 people:

Purchased?

  • 12 Corn Tortillas
  • 4 large Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Medium Red Onion
  • 1 bunch of Cilantro
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • 2 Large Ripe Avacados
  • 2 Limes
  • 1pd Ground Pork
  • Taco Seasoning (2 packages or the below ingredients)
    • 2 Teaspoons Cumin Seads
    • 1/4 Teaspoon Cardamon
    • 1/4 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
    • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 250 ml Sour Cream
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Recipe

1. Salsa

  • Dice 2 roma tomatoes and add salt to taste and place in a strainer to let some of the juice drip out.
  • Then chop 1/3 of the cilantro, finely dice 1/4 of the red onion, finely dice 1 jalapeno and mix together in a bowl.
  • Juice 1 lime and add to the avacado.
  • After 10 minutes add in the tomato while discarding the juice, and mix together with the other ingredints already in the bowl.
  • Taste and season again with salt if needed.

2. Guacamole

  • Remove the flesh of the avacado and coursely chop then place in a bowl.
  • Mix this together a bit more with a fork until partially smoooth with some chunks remaining.
  • Then finely chop the following: 1 jalapeno, 1/3 bunch of cilantro, 1 tomato, and 1/4 of the onion and mix together with teh avacado.

3. Seasoned Meat

  • Heat a medium skillet on med-high heat and add in 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil.
  • Chop the remaining 1/2 onion and add to the skillet when hot.
  • Then add in the Taco seasoning.
  • Dice the remaining tomato and add to the skillet.
  • You want to cook this mixture for 15 minutes to extract the flavour of the spices.
  • You can add 1 tablespoon of water at a time if needed in order to prevent sticking.
  • After 15 minutes have lapsed, add in the ground pork and stir until it is ready. Place in a bowl once cooked.

4. Tortillas (12)

  • If you have a BBQ or a gas stove, then toast the shells on both sides.
  • You can keep these warm by placing them in a bowl covered with a kitchen towel. (You will want the tortillas to be placed inside the towel so that they are not touching the bowl directly in order to maintain the heat.

5. Remaining Cilantro

  • Chop and place in a bowl.

6. Remaining 1 lime

  • Cut off the tips of each end.
  • Then cut in half width-wise and cut each half into 6 wedges.
  • This will give you 12 wedges to add a bit of additional lime juice to each taco.

7. Sour Cream

  • Mix with a spoon and place in another container for nicer presentation.

8. Serving

  • Serve all the ingredients on the table for you and your guests to enjoy this family style meal.
